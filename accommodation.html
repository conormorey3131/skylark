Accommodation

Space to relax, room for everyone

Four ensuite bedrooms, open-plan living and a designer kitchen — sleeping up to eight in comfort.

Skylark Lodge sleeps up to eight guests across four beautifully finished ensuite bedrooms — ideal for families, groups of friends or two couples sharing a Galway getaway.

Each bedroom has its own character and its own ensuite or private washroom, so there's no queuing in the morning. Downstairs, the open-plan kitchen, dining and living space brings everyone together, while the gardens and patio give you room to breathe.
