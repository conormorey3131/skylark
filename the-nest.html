The Nest · Elegant Oranmore Haven

A bright apartment for family & friends

Four bedrooms, open-plan living and a private garden — sleeping seven, just 8 minutes' walk from Oranmore village.

The Skylark Nest is an exquisite premium retreat at the edge of peaceful Oranmore — just an 8-minute walk from the vibrant village.

Set within the serene grounds of Skylark House, this apartment offers the height of modern luxury. It features four bedrooms, sleek open-plan living with a smart TV, high-speed Wi-Fi and a fully equipped kitchenette for effortless self-catering stays.

Sleeping up to seven, with air conditioning, an in-unit washer and dryer and free parking, it's ideal for families and groups. Stunning views of the manicured gardens invite you to step out onto your private patio and unwind amid the lush greenery.
