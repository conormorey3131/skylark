Welcome to Skylark Lodge

A rare kind of quiet, finished beautifully.

Tucked behind its own gates and framed by rolling green fields, Skylark Lodge is a spacious, light-filled home built for slow mornings and long, easy evenings. Natural stone, polished concrete floors, brass touches and soft natural light run throughout — a contemporary lodge with a warm, grounded soul.

Whether you've come for a family gathering, a countryside reset or simply somewhere wonderful to be together, every room has been thought through. Wake to birdsong, open the doors to the garden, and let the pace drop.

The setting

Wonderfully away from it all — yet never too far.

Skylark Lodge sits in the peaceful countryside of Glennascaul, County Galway — surrounded by green fields and open sky, with Galway city, the village of Oranmore and the wild Atlantic coast all within an easy drive.

  • Countryside walks right from the door
  • Beaches & the Atlantic coast a short drive away
  • Local pubs, food & markets nearby
  • Private parking within the gated grounds

Exact directions and local tips are shared by Breda when you book — just call or message to plan your stay.
